How To Play ?

  • You need to survive the horde of enemies
  • Use w , a , s , d to move upward , left , downward , right respectively.
  • To kill an enemy "left click" on them with your mouse.
  • Enemies will have different level of health.
  • Red enemy has 3 health
  • Orange enemy has 2 health
  • Yellow enemy has 1 health
  • Score as many point as you can by killing the enemies.
